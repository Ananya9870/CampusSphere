Admission Process

Join our college and take the first step towards a bright future. Here's how:

  1. Fill out the online application form on our official website.
  2. Submit required documents, including academic transcripts, identification proof, and recommendation letters.
  3. Attend an interview or entrance test as per the department's requirements.
  4. Wait for the evaluation of your application by our admissions team.
  5. Receive your admission confirmation along with details about the next steps.
  6. Complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees.
  7. Attend the orientation program to get acquainted with campus life and facilities.
